Intro to My Life

Miguel Renteria

My name is Miguel Renteria I am on of three children of Maria and Miguel Renteria. I have two other brothers and one half sister. My oldest brother is named Omar and my youngest is named Eduardo. I have never met my half sister as she lives in spain with her husband. Her name is Christy. We moved around a lot when I was growing up. We lived in Texas, New Mexico, Colorado, Mexico City and Las Vegas. I hated living in Las Vegas. Hate is a strong word so I will say this instead. Once I left I never looked back. Las Vegas will always hold a special place in my heart though. Most of my childhood was in Las Vegas and my family and best friends still live there. So even though I say I hate it, I don't really hate it and I go back often. LOL

I served in the US ARMY for 4 years. I was part of a special operations unit where our mission was to infiltrate enemy territory and set up for friendly's to have access to the warzone. My tasks and qualifications included:Being Airborne, Air Assault, and being able to clear buildings and large areas

My most recent accomplishment is being the first of my family to graduate from colleg. I graduated from the University of Hawaii at Manoa. Best college ever, I had so much fun and made great friends. Oh yeah I also got my degree.
